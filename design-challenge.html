Design Challenge

This page focuses on the specific design challenge that will be faced in this re-design.

What is the problem you're trying to solve?

To reach families with a member who has autism.

Frame it as a design question.

How does the Project Hope Foundation website reach families with autism and donors.

Now, state the ultimate impact you're trying to have.

To provide an organization that reaches people with autism and to give them the best chance at a successful life.

What are some possible solutions to your problem?

Organization of Call to Action buttons and limiting the use of them.

Limiting the overlap of images with text.

Providing a testimonial page for families on the home page.
