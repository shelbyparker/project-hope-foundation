Audience and Goals

This assignment led to determining the specific audience that this website is for, as well as, the main goals it is trying to achieve.

Determine the Audience

The audience that needs help ranges from all ages that have been diagnosed with autism. With autism being diagnosed as early as 18 months old, the website needs to reach parents and guardians as well as teens and adults all the way to the elderly who feel they need help with their own struggle with autism. With the spectrum being so spread out those that are daignosed may have very different needs. With all of this, the site needs to vary.

After the immediate audience directly affected by autism there is a need to reach those that will want to volunteer and possibly donate to the Project Hope Foundation. This process began by considering who would be the people able to help the diagnosed the best. I found that teachers and therapists are key to the transformation of these people's lives. After that comes volunteers of parents and potentially high school students earning volunteer hours. These people not only affect the autistic students, but it brings new light and hope to their own lives.

Lastly, I identified the skeptics. These are the people who feel autism does not even exist or that people who have this diagnosis do not deserve special attention to learn skills that would benefit them in life.

Overarching Goals

Client

  • Raise awareness about who they are.
  • Reach people who live with autism.
  • Easily connect families to the organization.
  • Show results from what the organization does.
  • Receive money and classroom materials for teaching and daily use.
  • Partner with corportations for supplies.
  • Distribute resources to each age group within the organization.
  • Gain traffic on the website.

Audience

  • Learn more about the Project Hope Foundation.
  • Watch, listen, or read about testimonials from families who participate in the organization.
  • Learn how to become a volunteer.
  • Connect with Project Hope.
  • Give donations.
  • Spread the word to people around about Project Hope and invite people to learn more.

Website

  • Provide clear information to each directed audience.
  • Gain families and people with autism.
  • Gain volunteeers for the organization.
  • Provide a brief description of what autism is.
  • Provide testimonials for the audience.
  • Include an events section.
  • Include updated news articles.
  • Make contact information easy to find.

Specific Goals

User Goal Content/Action
Parent/Guardian Donate, volunteer, and spread awareness and support. Donate money, fill out volunteer form, spend time with students at the facility.
Therapists Donate, Volunteer, Educate Families. Host an event for families to learn more about autism.
Teachers Volunteer, Donate. Donate classroom supplies and educational books, fillout volunteer form, contact to become a teacher at the organization.
High School Students Volunteer, learn more about autism, learn to care for those that may be different than oneself. Fill out volunteer form, spend time with students at the organization.
Schools Donate supplies and resources relating to classrooms with autistic students. Donate curriculum, tables, toys, special seating.
